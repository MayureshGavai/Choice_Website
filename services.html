W Wedding Planning

Wedding planner and stylist for weddings in Europe Whether you're planning an exclusive event or an intimate ceremony, it's all about what matters to you as a couple, not traditions or trends. That's why we are dedicated to creating a personal celebration that is telling your story.

As your wedding planners and stylists, we are the ones fully focused on creating timelines, going through vendor agreements and checking floor plans so you don't have to. Instead, you can focus on the fun parts; decorations, themes and final touches. Forget all about the trends and traditions, we are here to plan the wedding perfectly tailored to you.
