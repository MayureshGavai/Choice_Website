A About us

Wedding agency Choice was founded in 2016 by Mariia Pinchuk who wanted to offer couples mindful planning and create celebrations fully focused on their personalities, stories and wishes, giving them an opportunity to start their marriages in the best way possible!

Over the years we have planned and styled weddings worldwide and we continue to treat each celebration with the same dedication, enthusiasm and professionalism it deserves. It would be an honour to plan yours!

We value qualitative design.Rather than adding too much we aim to find the golden middle line and work with quality over quantity. The world becomes a little bit better if we surround ourselves with beauty, according to what we believe!

M

Meet Mariia

I'm a founder and planner of Choice. I want you to have a celebration that is natural, beautiful and full of personality. I want you to enjoy the planning journey and fall even deeper in love with each other during it.

For me, there is nothing more fantastic than when you share your love story and wishes for the wedding with me. I am dedicated to guide and encourage you so you feel calm and inspired all the way through

With empathy, kindness and professionalism I'm creating a celebration you and your guests will remember forever. We work together and I will be involved as much or as little as you wish

After having worked in every aspect of the event industry on an international level it was the realisation I could combine my love for people's stories, beautiful objects and planning that I set up Choice.
